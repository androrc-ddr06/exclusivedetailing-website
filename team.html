Our Story

Built on the Belief That Every Car Deserves the Best.

Exclusive Detailing started with a simple frustration: the detailers we trusted kept cutting corners. So we stopped looking for the right crew and became one. We trained, invested in professional-grade products, and built a service around one standard — do it right, every time, no exceptions.

We are based in the Sacramento region and proud to serve the communities we live in. When your neighbor recommends us, we take that personally. That's why you'll never see us rush a job or leave a vehicle less than perfect.

Our team is small by design. Two people on every job means two sets of eyes, full accountability, and a result we stand behind with every appointment.
